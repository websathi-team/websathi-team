# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

At WebSathi, we are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone who contributes to our projects.

We expect all contributors to uphold this code of conduct in all interactions.

---

## ✅ Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Collaborate professionally
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences

---

## ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Personal attacks or insults
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Publishing others’ private information without permission
- Any behavior that violates professional standards

---

## 🛠️ Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to:
- Remove inappropriate content
- Temporarily or permanently ban contributors who violate this code
- Take appropriate corrective action

---

## 📫 Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to:

📧 **Email:** info@websathi.net  

All reports will be reviewed confidentially and handled professionally.

---

## ❤️ Commitment

By participating in WebSathi projects, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct and help maintain a positive community.
